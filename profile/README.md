# Pup Pup Games 🐾

### Silly games for serious fun.

We're a small studio making board games, tabletop RPG material, and video games. Some are built purely to entertain. Others carry a real educational payload underneath the play. The bar for every one of them is the same: **it has to be genuinely fun first.**

---

## 🎲 What we make

Everything we ship falls into one of two tracks:

- **Pure Entertainment** — games whose only job is to be a great time at the table.
- **Serious Fun** — games that teach something real while staying fun to play. The lesson is woven into the mechanics, never bolted on as a quiz. If the lesson ever makes the game less fun, the lesson loses.

## 🤖 How we make our games

We're a tiny team, and AI tools are part of how we design, write, and produce what we make. For a studio our size, they're the difference between an idea staying a sketch and an idea becoming a game you can actually play. Every title still clears the same bar: real people play it, judge it, and ship it only when it's genuinely fun.

We're here to make great games, not to relitigate the politics of how they're made. If that's a dealbreaker for you, we genuinely understand, and our games probably aren't the right fit. For everyone else: pull up a chair. The fun is the point.
